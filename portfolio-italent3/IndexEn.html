About myself

My name is Florian Landuyt. I am going to tell something more about how I've started the bachelor Applied Information Technology at HoGent.
My way to this point at school hasn't been without obstacles. From the moment I can remember, I've always been busy with sports. Because of this, I started doing gymnastics from young age. Because it was only possible to continue this at the Voskenslaan, I started going to this school. I was selected to participate elite gymnastics. When I took this big decision in my life, I had not tought about the fact I didn't had many choices in which direction I was going to go. This was also the same case when I had to choose which direction in the second degree in secondary education. In the following years I continued doing Sience - topsport.
When I finished secondary education without failing any class, I was finally able to choose whatever direction I wanted to go, which I still didn't do. Because of the fact I had chosen the last six year for sciences, I felt obligated to go a little bit further down this road. After another two years of doing two direction that include sciences, I finally made the decision of doing something completely different. This is now my second year in Applied Information Technology at HoGent en I'm still very happy of my choice. Despite having sometimes difficulties with the combination topsport-studying, I'm convinces of calling myself a Informaticus in a couple of years.

What have accomplished?

In this direction I follow at school, I didn't accomplish anything in the workfield. Before I started doing informatics, I never got in touch with this type of work. In the two years I've been going to school at HoGent, I've finished one project. This was my first contact with a big groupwork, where each groupmember had to make their own input and was responsible for doing his part. This project took a lot of deadlines with it, causing I learned how to handle precious time. I learned that in the future, projects will take a lot of difficulties with them. When I'm not at school, you can find me in the gymnastics hall, where I'm doing gymnastics. Since my five years, I've been realy active with this sport. I've qualified myself in 2015 for the final at the world championships. My sportcarreer has ensured that I'm really disciplined. What I also managed to control because of being a high level gymnast, is that I learned to handle a really huge pressure.
